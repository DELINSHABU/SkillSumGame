🎨 Design System Overview

"Soft Arcade" aesthetic — Playful but not childish. Clean but not cold. Rewarding but not overwhelming.

Design Philosophy — "Tactile Joy"
Warmth
Soft pinks, rounded corners, gentle shadows
Precision
8px grid, consistent spacing, clean alignment
Delight
Spring animations, particle bursts, sound-visual sync
Clarity
High contrast questions, no clutter in game view
Progress
XP bars, stars, streak flames everywhere
Key Rules: No 1px borders (use surface shifts). No pure black. No flat buttons (use 3D shadow blocks). No standard easing (use spring/elastic). Every pixel molded from clay.
